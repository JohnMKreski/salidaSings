Come Sing With Us!

The Noteables are recruiting female singers for their winter concert series, “Peace On Earth." on December 13-15. The Noteables are a chorus consisting of 25-30 women. We are a compassionate, friendly and supportive group of singers. We cheer each other on, help out when someone gets sick and have awesome rehearsals, learning vocal technique while having fun. Let’s face it. If it isn’t fun why do it?


Thursday night rehearsals start September 5, 5:30-7:30 pm at Salida United Methodist Church (diagonally across from the library) at the corner of D and 4th Streets, Salida. Due to Halloween and Thanksgiving falling on Thursdays, those weeks the rehearsals will be on Tuesdays, October 29 and November 26.



The Noteables will perform a wide variety of music that will encompass not only the Christmasy/winter related songs but also 5 songs about peace and unity, reflecting all the genuine sentiment hidden beneath the commercialism of the holiday. The concerts will include a wide selection of genres: gospel, unusual arrangements of traditional carols, winter ballads, calypso and Celtic.


Songs include: The First Noel with Pachebel’s Canon, Sing Joy To the World, Chapua Kali Desemba, a rhythmic African carol with both English and easy to learn African lyrics, A Gospel Song of Peace, based loosely on the Dona, Nobis, Pacem round, the lively Let Heaven And Nature Sing, which will get you dancing, The Work of Christmas, reminding us that the Christmas spirit continues throughout the year, Christmas Pipes, a Celtic song by Celtic Woman…. plus many more.


The chorus will be accompanied by Oso Blanco on drums, Kate Chiras on electric bass and Brenda Covert on flute. Featured will be Dave Tipton playing several instrumental carols on his Chapman Stick.



Concert Dates:
  • Friday, December 13, 7:00pm at Clearview Church in Buena Vista
  • Saturday, December 14, 7:00pm at Salida SteamPlant Event Center
  • Sunday, December 15, 3:00pm at Salida SteamPlant Event Center

  • Tuesday, December 3rd at Salida SteamPlant Event Center

    Dues: Sliding scale $30.00-$75.00 per season with financial assistance from our treasury.

    Absences: 3 allowed with a 4th for emergencies.

    Recordings: We'll provide free practice CDs or flash drives for all the songs. For $5.00 extra you will be provided with a parts recording for all the songs with the director playing the piano and singing your part separate from the whole chorus. Music packets, a complete schedule and recordings will be ready for pick-up in mid-August


Remember that music is healing and has the ability to bring perfect strangers together in laughter, joy and harmony, something that will be front and center come this fall. Please join us. Spread the word! Don’t be shy. You’ll love it!!
