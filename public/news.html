We Can't Stop Singing!

The Noteables will present their spring concert series, "Make Space For Life To Live", May 3-5.


Under the animated direction of Linda Taylor, the chorus will perform a wide variety of music that reflects the theme of making space for opportunities in our busy lives: allowing ourselves to make U-turns when things don’t work out, being brave enough to embrace the unknown and loving life enough to explore the mysteries before us.


We start out with Robert Frost’s The Road Not Taken and a delightful traveling song called Heading Home. The familiar, Morning Has Broken, shines light and possibility on Josh Groban’s powerful, The Mystery of Your Gift and encourages you to Grow Your Garden.


A trio introduces The Mighty Oak with a retelling of childhood memories and Yufen Chou, violinist, soothes us with the beautiful ballad, from Ken Burns’ Civil War documentary, Ashokan Farewell.


The mood changes with the hilarious Painless Opera involving all kinds of theatrical antics. Paul Simon’s Feelin’ Groovy, Livin’ Life in 4/4 Time and a dramatic version of Defying Gravity from Broadway’s Wicked bring even more excitement to this versatile program.


The show will feature guest instrumentalists: Oso Blanco, drums; Yufen Chou, violin; Kathleen Chiras, electric bass; and Brenda Covert, flute.


Performances:

  • Friday, May 3, 7:00pm at Clearview Church in Buena Vista
  • Saturday, May 4, 7:00pm at Salida SteamPlant Event Center
  • Sunday, May 5, 3:00pm at Salida SteamPlant Event Center

Please join us for our spring concert series. This is an opportunity to “Make Space For Life To Live” Don’t be shy. You’ll love them!
