Don’t Miss Our Christmas Concerts!

The Noteables are preparing for their Christmas concert series, “We Are The Lights”, December 12-14.


Under the animated direction of Linda Taylor, the chorus will perform a wide variety of seasonal music, including unusual arrangements of Christmas carols, winter ballads, humorous selections and songs of peace, light, and love.



Songs will include: We start out with The Little Drummer Boy, partnered with Peace On Earth, I Saw Three Ships, Dormi, Dormi, a beautiful lullaby in Latin and Something Told the Wild Geese, the poignant story of their winter flight and Carol of Winter Peace. Lively selections include Sing We Now Of Christmas, Rise and Shine! We Are the Lights, the hilarious One Of Those Summer Winters…. plus, many more!

Concert dates:

  • Friday, December 12th, 7:00pm at Mountain Heights Church, Buena Vista
  • Saturday, December 13th, 7:00pm at Salida SteamPlant
  • Sunday, December 14th, 3:00pm at Salida SteamPlant



The Noteables are a chorus consisting of 25-30 women. We are a compassionate, friendly, and supportive group of singers whose goal is to bring high quality performances to Chaffee County. Our music inspires, uplifts and moves audiences to laughter, tears and unexplored insights. Don’t miss out! Let us put you in the mood for Christmas!
